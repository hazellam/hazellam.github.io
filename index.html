Who Am I?

Hi! I'm Hazel Lam, a Chinese-American, second year Psychology and Public Health major at the Universtiy of California, Berkeley. I'm an aspiring Occupational Therapist with a demonstrated background in problem-solving and strong interpersonal skills.

As a teenager, I immigrated from Hong Kong to California with my family. I grew up with my brother and attended school in San Mateo. During my free time, I play volleyball, make DIY projects, volunteer at my local Red Cross chapter, and play with my cat!

In the long run, I hope to open my own private practice in Occupational Therapy. I'm driven by my ability to help people develop, recover, and improve everyday skills that can make their quality of life better.
